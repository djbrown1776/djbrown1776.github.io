About
Background

I'm Daniel Brown — an analytics engineer relocating to Germany, with 4+ years of experience turning messy data into modeled, trustworthy analytics.

My background is in BI and analytics at an energy company, where I spent 4+ years automating Environmental reporting — building SQL transformation logic in Redshift and delivering reporting layers that stakeholders across the organization relied on daily. I've since expanded into full-stack data engineering: Python ingestion, containerized cloud deployments, infrastructure-as-code with Terraform, dbt modeling, and Airflow orchestration.

I hold a business administration degree with an MIS concentration, and I learn best by building. I'm particularly drawn to the energy sector — especially opportunities in clean energy and the Energiewende — where good data infrastructure can drive real impact.
